Chicken with Sweet-Sour Sauce

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken breast 500g
Sweet-sour sauce 1/2 cup
Bell peppers 2 (sliced)
Pineapple chunks 1 cup
Onion 1 (sliced)

Steps:

  • Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and sauté in a skillet until golden.
  • Add sliced onions, bell peppers, and pineapple chunks to the skillet and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Pour in the sweet-sour sauce, stir well, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice.

Beshbarmak

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredient Quantity
Beef or lamb 1 kg
Onions 2 (sliced)
Flat noodles 500g
Salt To taste
Bay leaf 1

Steps:

  • Boil the beef or lamb with a bay leaf and salt for 1.5 hours, skimming off the foam.
  • Prepare the flat noodles by boiling them in salted water.
  • Slice the boiled meat and mix with cooked noodles and sautéed onions.
  • Serve with a side of broth and garnish with fresh herbs.
